WELCOME TO TAPESTRY YOGA STUDIO

Nestled along the tranquil banks of the River Shannon in Carrick-on-Shannon, our yoga studio offers a serene sanctuary where ancient wisdom meets modern wellness. Our bright, spacious studio creates the perfect atmosphere for transformation, providing a peaceful escape from daily life where you can breathe, stretch, and connect with yourself and others. Whether you're seeking physical renewal, mental clarity, or spiritual growth, our welcoming space invites you to embark on a journey of self-discovery and holistic wellness in the heart of Ireland's north-west region.

LIST OF SERVICES PROVIDED

  • Yoga Classes - Group sessions led by certified instructors teaching various yoga styles and levels
  • Guided Meditation - Mindfulness sessions led by experienced practitioners focusing on breath awareness and inner peace
  • Room Rental - Clean, serene spaces available for private events, workshops, and group activities
  • One-On-One Sessions - Personalized yoga instruction tailored to individual needs and goals
